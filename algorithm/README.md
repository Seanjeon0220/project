The difficulty is not spread evenly, and that is the point. `math` and `strings` are wide and shallow — a lot of Bronze problems, worked through for volume. `search_and_traversal`, `dp`, and `data_structure` are where the harder problems live: graph traversal is more than half Gold or above, and dynamic programming is close behind.

The two largest categories, `math` and `implementation_etc`, are large because solved.ac itself does not subdivide them.
